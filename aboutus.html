Since its founding in 1991 Armstrong Transmitter Corporation has developed a solid track record for providing reliable and easily maintainable RF systems to the Broadcast Industry.

Armstrong's innovation and design advancements continue into the "Digital Age of Radio Broadcasting". Our engineering team developed the first digital amplifiers used to prove the feasibility of the new Digital FM IBOC technology in the "real world" broadcast environment.

This research and development lead to the eventual authorization of the IBOC technology by the FCC and helped *HD Radio evolve from a theory into a practical approach for digital broadcasting in the U.S.

While our innovation and design advancements allow Armstrong to help advance the broadcast technology into the digital era, we remains a solidly based, customer-oriented company.

Armstrong's founder and President Sinan Mimaroglu's desire to provide excellent quality, high reliability, easily maintained broadcast RF Systems and exceptional customer support continues to be our focus on a daily basis.

Our company focus has allowed us to become one of only two transmitter manufacturers authorized by the National Weather Service to provide transmitters to the NOAA, Weather Radio Network in support of their life saving mission.

In addition Armstrong had provided over 800 FM transmitters of various configurations and power levels to broadcasters throughout the United States and the World. We credit the growing acceptance of our products to the increasing recognition of our exceptional quality, high reliability, leading edge features and some of the best customer support available in the industry.
