Services

What we offer

Medical

We specialize in all types of medical accounts, including private practices, hospital divisions, nursing agencies, and pharmaceutical delivery companies.

Commercial

Using your website, we're able to process orders by your customers, allowing a simple phone-order solution for a web-based business.

Voice Mail

Your phone tree is customizable and allows multiple outgoing messages, enabling you to make sure your clients get the information they need.



Order Entry

While ordering your products on line is the most convenient way possible for many of your customers, this won't be the case for all of them. Some people still don't have ready access to the internet and/or are not comfortable using it. We can help you to assist these customers.

Paging Service

Action Telephone Exchange offers both alpha numeric and digital pagers. Our paging system covers the United States, including Hawaii and Alaska. Alpha paging is a good way to save time and money. They prevent unnecessary callbacks by having messages sent right to your pager.

Overflow Service

With overflow service your calls will be forwarded to us during the day once they reach the predetermined number of rings, all lines are busy, or in any other circumstance in which your voicemail would normally kick in.

  • We offer patching service upon request, connecting callers to your home or private office number without revealing it to the caller. We will also patch you to any number you request, removing the possibility of your number appearing on Caller ID.

  • Messages can be delivered via live operator, fax, pager, voicemail, or e-mail.

  • Our sophisticated voicemail system assists in call screening, saving you money by handling routine messages without the need for a live operator.

  • Our customers use an express check-in line, which is seldom placed on hold. Custom check-in lines are available.
